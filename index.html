Overview

This workshop will focus on the latest advances in semantic-aware navigation and mapping, exploring how robots can evolve toward cognitive and adaptive behaviors. It aims to bring together researchers from robotics, AI, and related fields to discuss algorithms, systems, and applications that push the boundaries of perception-driven autonomy.
